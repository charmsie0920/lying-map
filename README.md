# Lying Map

It’s a map of the world, but it actively lies to you. 

## What is this?
Basically, you look at a map. You hover over a country, and it tells you it’s a completely different country. 

Your job is to figure out what kind of twisted logic the map is using to lie to you. Are the countries swapped alphabetically? Are they stealing their neighbor's identity? Who knows. 

Once you think you've cracked the cipher, you prove it by typing the expected fake name into the one "quarantined" country on the map. 

If you get it right, you get points. If you get it wrong, you feel mildly foolish. It’s not that deep.

## How to play it on your machine
You probably know the drill. Put this in your terminal:
```bash
npm install
npm start
```
have fun. or don't
